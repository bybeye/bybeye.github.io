About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Australian National University, working closely with Professor Sylvie Thiebaux, an AAAI Fellow and Co-Editor-in-Chief of the Artificial Intelligence journal. I earned my PhD from The University of Sydney, under the supervision of Professor Tongliang Liu, Director of the Sydney AI Centre, and Professor Dadong Wang, Principal Research Scientist at CSIRO Data61.

My research interests focus on weakly supervised learning, representation learning, and neural-symbolic learning.

Before starting my PhD, I co-founded the agricultural IoT company ACSM in China with Mr. Hai Lan, leading a development team of 50 staff from 2010 to 2018. Our mission was to enhance food quality by optimizing agricultural practices, improving resource management, boosting farmers' income, and promoting sustainable farming.
