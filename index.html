About me

I am Yingbin Bai, a PhD student at the University of Sydney. My supervisor is Prof. Tongliang Liu. Prior to joining the University of Sydney, I gained several years of industry experience, where I focused on utilizing IT techniques to solve agriculture problems. I strongly believe that machine learning will play a crucial role in addressing agricultural issues. During my free time, I enjoy traveling and photography.

My research interests encompass weakly supervised learning and unsupervised representation learning. If you have any ideas or encounter any problems related to these areas, I would be more than happy to discuss and collaborate.
